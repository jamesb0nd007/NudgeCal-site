Frequently Asked Questions

Notifications aren't showing up

Make sure NudgeCal has both Calendar and Notification permissions granted. Check Settings > Apps > NudgeCal > Permissions on your device. Also ensure battery optimization is disabled for NudgeCal.

Events from some calendars don't appear

Go to NudgeCal Settings > Handled Calendars and make sure all the calendars you want to monitor are selected.

Snooze isn't working

NudgeCal uses exact alarms for reliable snooze. On Android 12+, make sure "Alarms & reminders" permission is enabled for NudgeCal in your device settings.

How do I delete my data?

Go to NudgeCal Settings and use "Reset to defaults" to clear all preferences. You can also uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data. NudgeCal never sends data off your device.
