What is NudgeCal?

NudgeCal replaces Android's default calendar notification system with better controls, snooze options, and a cleaner experience. Your events, your way.

Key Features

  • Flexible snooze presets with custom durations
  • Quiet hours to silence alerts on your schedule
  • Per-event muting for noisy recurring meetings
  • Reminder re-firing so you never miss an event
  • Calendar color-coded notifications
  • Support for multiple calendars
  • No internet required — everything stays on your device
